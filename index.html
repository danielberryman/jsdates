JavaScript Dates

Do you know what timestamp a Date object in JavaScript will return?

This app will generate a Date constructor in JavaScript. It will then ask you what year, month, date, and hour will be returned when local methods or UTC methods are called on the Date object. Good luck! Also, this is a JS editor so feel free to manipulate whatever you want! But BEWARE...if you click generate all will disappear and you'll see a new Date problem.
